Landscaping and plant care

When conducting landscaping, it is necessary to first develop a dendroplan that will include the volume of landscaping, an assortment of trees and shrubs, flower crops and lawn areas, the style of landscape design. It is also necessary to carry out soil analysis on the site, to schematically determine the places of planting of plants taking into account the location of houses, structures, paths.At the design stage, the climatic conditions of the territory are taken into account, comparing them with the requirements of the plants that are optimally rooted for them, because even the most beautiful and expensive landscaping is worthless if the plants become unfit after one year of planting. It also takes into account the practicality and rational use of the area, accompanied by the planting of fruit trees, which not only fit perfectly in the exterior, but also bring good tasty and nutritious fruits.
